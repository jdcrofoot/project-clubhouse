Project Clubhouse Brand System – Official Visual Direction

The attached images are not concept sketches.

They are now the official visual direction for Project Clubhouse and should become the foundation of the website, marketing materials, future pitch decks, and in-app marketing.

Treat these images as the beginning of our Brand System.

Do not regenerate them.

Do not reinterpret them.

Do not replace them with stock photography or AI-generated alternatives.

Instead, use them as the approved creative direction and integrate them into the website and future marketing assets.

Two Levels of Vision

The attached imagery intentionally includes two complementary concepts.

1. Dream Vision

These images represent the long-term vision.

They communicate:

the feeling
the architecture
the atmosphere
the emotional experience
the community
the energy
the culture

These are not literal construction drawings.

They answer:

"What are we ultimately trying to build?"

Use them to inspire every design decision.

2. Pilot Club

The second set represents a realistic first location.

This version intentionally simplifies the concept while preserving the same feeling.

The pilot should feel:

attainable
financially realistic
operationally efficient
community focused

The pilot is not "less ambitious."

It is simply Phase One.

The long-term vision remains the north star.

Design Philosophy

Every design decision should reinforce this statement:

Fitness is the invitation.

Community is the reason people stay.

The app exists to strengthen the physical clubhouse.

The clubhouse exists to strengthen the community.

Architectural Direction

Preserve the existing visual language:

industrial materials
black architecture
warm woods
concrete
steel
subtle neon green accents
premium lighting
clean signage
sportsbook inspiration
modern university commons
high-performance training environment

Avoid:

luxury wellness spa
sterile commercial gym
nightclub
Buffalo Wild Wings
esports arena
overly corporate coworking spaces

The goal is modern, authentic, energetic, and welcoming.

Photography Direction

The attached images define our photography style.

Photography should always feel:

candid
documentary
cinematic
warm
authentic
lived-in

Avoid:

posed fitness models
influencer photography
people looking directly into the camera
obvious stock imagery

People should appear to be genuinely living their lives.

Representation

Across all future imagery maintain broad representation.

Include:

men
women
teenagers
college students
young professionals
parents
older mentors
different ethnic backgrounds
different body types
different fitness levels

This diversity should feel natural rather than staged.

The Gym

The Performance Floor remains the heart of the building.

It should always communicate:

hard work

progress

encouragement

mentorship

personal growth

Show:

spotting

teaching

celebrating PRs

beginners

experienced athletes

friends training together

The Clubhouse

The Clubhouse is now the emotional center.

It should feel like:

the place people naturally gather.

Show:

watch parties

friends talking

students studying

parents working

fantasy football drafts

protein shakes

coffee

community tables

conversation

The clubhouse should never feel empty.

The Fuel Station

This is not a café.

This is not a restaurant.

This is affordable healthy fuel.

Show:

protein

coffee

grab-and-go meals

hydration

members talking while eating

speed

simplicity

affordability

Study Areas

Show:

students

college work

tutoring

group projects

remote work

The study space should support community, not replace it.

Outdoor Space

watch parties

App Integration

The website should integrate the updated PrimalTrack experience.

Focus on:

Progress

Compete

Your People

Circles

Link Ups

Presence

The app should be presented as the digital layer connecting the physical clubhouse.

Website Direction

The website should become more cinematic.

Use:

larger imagery

less text

more white space

stronger emotional headlines

immersive scrolling

Let the imagery tell the story.

Important

Every future design decision should pass one simple test:

Does this make someone wish they had a Project Clubhouse in their town?

If the answer is yes, it belongs.

If the answer is no, reconsider the direction.

The attached images are now the visual benchmark for the Project Clubhouse brand. Preserve their tone, authenticity, and sense of belonging as the website and product continue to evolve.
