We're building something much bigger than a gym.

This project is currently called Project Clubhouse (working title). It is a fitness-focused community company built around the idea that the future of fitness is not just exercise—it's belonging.

I'm attaching a series of Vision Boards that represent our current thinking. These are not final designs. They are intended to communicate the vision and provide a foundation that we will continue iterating on.

This project is being built alongside my 13-year-old son, Grant. One of my goals is to create something that genuinely appeals to teenagers, young adults, and parents—not by guessing what they want, but by designing it together.

The Mission

Create the place people don't want to leave.

Traditional gyms are places where people work out and leave.

We want to create a modern fitness clubhouse where people:

Train
Improve
Build friendships
Study
Watch games
Attend events
Celebrate progress
Feel like they belong

The workout is only the beginning.

Community is the product.

The Target Customer

Primary:

Teenagers (13–18)
High school athletes
College students
Young adults

Secondary:

Parents
Adults who enjoy fitness and community
Families

Our goal is to create a place where a parent is excited to pay approximately $40/month because they know their child is spending time in a healthy, motivating environment.

Our Biggest Differentiator

We are NOT building another leaderboard app.

We are building a motivation platform.

Most fitness platforms reward the strongest.

We want to reward the people who consistently improve.

Progress is the product.
