We've made tremendous progress on the visual design. The website is beginning to feel like a real company instead of just a concept.

Do not redesign the visual identity. The current aesthetic (black background, neon green accents, bold typography, industrial feel) is the direction we want to continue.

Instead, I want to evolve the site from a gym landing page into a product experience that communicates why Project Clubhouse is fundamentally different.

Overall Goal

Someone visiting the website should understand within 30 seconds that:

This is NOT another gym.
This is a fitness community.
The physical club and the app work together.
Members are rewarded for progress, consistency, and community—not just strength.
This is a place where people belong.

9. Website Philosophy

Do NOT build a traditional gym website.

We are building a modern product website similar to companies like:

Apple

Nike

Notion

Whoop

Oura

The site should feel like a technology-enabled community brand.

10. Design Principles

Maintain:

Black backgrounds

Neon green accents

Industrial architecture

Simple layouts

Large typography

Premium but affordable

Modern

Authentic

Do not introduce luxury finishes or overly polished health-club aesthetics.

The feeling should be:

"A place where ambitious people want to spend time."

Final Direction

Every page should answer one question:

Why would someone choose to spend three hours here instead of one?

Every section should reinforce that Project Clubhouse is not selling gym access.

We are creating a community where people improve together.

Build every future enhancement around that principle.
